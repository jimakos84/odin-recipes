Greek salad

This greek salad will cure your sadness

Ingredients

  • 2 tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • salt
  • 1 cucumber, small
  • 1/2 green bell pepper
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 20g capers
  • 50g olives
  • 150g feta
  • pepper
  • oregano
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil

Let's get down to business

  1. Cut the tomatoes in half and remove the stem. Cut them in small pieces and place them in a bowl. Add sald and put aside
  2. In another bowl mix the honey with 2 tablespoons of red wine vinegar, until the honey melts. Then add to the tomatoes
  3. Cut the cucumber in half and remove the middle. Chop in half-moon shape and add to the tomatoes
  4. Cut the pepper in small strips and add to the rest of the vegetables
  5. Cut the onion into really thin slices and add it to a bowl. Add 1 tablespoon of red wine vinegar and mix it. Add it to the rest
  6. Add the capers, the olives, the feta, pepper, oregano and olive oil and serve
  7. GET SOME BREAD TO DIP
