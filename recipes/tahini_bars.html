Tahini bars

Packed with energy, these bars are an excellent option to take with you on a hike

Ingredients

  • 450g tahini, or peanut butter
  • 200g honey
  • 200g chocolate couverture, cut into pieces
  • 150g nuts
  • 150g whole-grain cereal
  • 200g oats
  • 80g cranberries, dried

Let's make these badboys

  1. Heat the honey in the microwave for 30 seconds.
  2. Combine the tahini and honey together in a large bowl. Be careful not to overmix because it will make the mixture too thick.
  3. Put the chocolate in a bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and melt in the microwave for 2 minutes, set at 800 watts. (Or you can melt it in a bain marie.)
  4. Add the melted chocolate to the tahini-honey mixture and mix.
  5. Add the oats, mixed nuts and cranberries. Mix with a spatula. Add the cereal and continue to mix by hand.
  6. Spread a very large piece of plastic wrap over a 20x30 cm baking pan, letting it hang over the edges. Spread the mixture over the wrap and sprinkle some more cereal over the top. Cover with overhanging plastic wrap.
  7. Refrigerate for 3-4 hours, until firm.
  8. To serve, cut into 20 equal sized bars, each 5 cm thick.
